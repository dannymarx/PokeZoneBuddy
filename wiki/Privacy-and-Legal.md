# Privacy and Legal

PokeZoneBuddy is an independent fan-made companion app. This page summarises licensing, attribution, and how the app handles your data.

## Data Handling
- PokeZoneBuddy stores events, cities, spots, timelines, and reminder preferences locally on your devices using SwiftData.
- No personal accounts or external databases are required. Exported files stay wherever you choose to share or store them.
- Network requests are limited to fetching public Pokémon GO event data from the ScrapedDuck API.

## Third-Party Data Sources
- **ScrapedDuck API** (MIT License) supplies structured event information that mirrors LeekDuck updates.
- **LeekDuck.com** is the original content source, used with permission for informational purposes.
