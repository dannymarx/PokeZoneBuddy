# Support and Feedback

Have an idea, spotted a bug, or need help importing a plan? Here’s how to get in touch and keep the community informed.

## Report Issues
- Open a GitHub issue in the main repository with:
  - A clear description of what happened.
  - Steps to reproduce the problem.
  - Your platform (macOS or iOS) and app version (see Settings → About).
  - Screenshots or exported files if they help explain the issue.
- Tag whether the issue relates to Events, Timeline Planner, Cities, Notifications, or Import/Export to speed up triage.

## Feature Requests
- Start a discussion or feature request issue outlining:
  - The problem you want to solve.
  - Why the current workflow falls short.
  - Any mockups or external references that illustrate your idea.
- Popular suggestions may be prioritised in upcoming releases.

## Contributing
- Check the repository’s contribution guidelines for coding standards and localisation tips.
- Review open issues or tagged “help wanted” items to see where you can pitch in.
- Keep pull requests focused on a single feature or fix, and link back to the related issue.

## Stay in the Loop
- Watch the repository or subscribe to releases to be notified when new builds ship.
- Review the [Changelog](https://github.com/dannymarx/PokeZoneBuddy/blob/main/CHANGELOG.md) for detailed release notes once they publish.

Need to understand the app’s legal stance before contributing? Read [Privacy and Legal](./Privacy-and-Legal).
