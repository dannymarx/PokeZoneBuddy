Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 1, 2025

Introduction

PokeZoneBuddy ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

PokeZoneBuddy is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information:

  • Favorite Cities: The cities you select are stored locally on your device only
  • Event Data: We fetch Pokémon GO event data from the ScrapedDuck API

Data Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device using Apple's native storage frameworks (UserDefaults/SwiftData). We do not transmit, store, or have access to any of your personal information on our servers.

Third-Party Services

PokeZoneBuddy uses the following third-party services:

  • ScrapedDuck API: We fetch public Pokémon GO event information from ScrapedDuck, which sources data from LeekDuck.com. This API does not collect or store any personal information from our users.

No Analytics or Tracking

We do not use any analytics, tracking, or advertising services. Your usage of the app is completely private.

No Account Required

PokeZoneBuddy does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information such as your name, email address, or location.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. Since we do not collect any personal information at all, the app is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

Data Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • You can delete all data by deleting the app
  • You can modify your data at any time within the app
  • No data leaves your device except for fetching public event information
